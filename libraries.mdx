---
title: 'Libraries included with PillarX'
---

With PillarX we already have a number of npm packages included
to ease the process for devs wanting to build apps on the platform.


* [@emotion/react 11.11.3](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@emotion/react)
* [@emotion/styled 11.11.0](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@emotion/styled)
* [@etherspot/transaction-kit 0.7.0](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@etherspot/transaction-kit)
* [@mui/joy 5.0.0-beta.22](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@mui/joy)
* [@react-spring/web 9.7.3](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@react-spring/web)
* [axios 1.6.5](https://www.npmjs.com/package/axios)
* [ethers 5.7.2](https://www.npmjs.com/package/ethers)
* [react-google-charts 4.0.1](https://www.npmjs.com/package/react-google-charts)
* [react-icons 4.12.0](https://www.npmjs.com/package/react-icons)
* [react-transition-group 4.4.5](https://www.npmjs.com/package/react-transition-group)
* [styled-components 6.1.1](https://www.npmjs.com/package/styled-components)
* [webfontloader 1.6.28](https://www.npmjs.com/package/webfontloader)

During development you can also add more packages which your app may require.
