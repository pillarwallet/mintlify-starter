PillarX is a multichain ecosystem of web3 dapps powered by 
Account Abstraction to let users access a crypto playground 
with an unparallelled user experience.	

With a floating action bar at the bottom of the application,
users will always have access to a list of apps, your account, 
the ability to send assets, and transaction history, at all times.

PillarX will have the concept of an "app store" that is a 
central place to discover apps where there's something for 
everyone - Defi protocols, socialfi, utilities, games, and much more! 

Each Dapp is an isolated React component which makes [integrating](/integration)
new apps incredibly simple.
